What did you hope to learn and gain from this internship?

What I hoped for most was to grow in my ability to explain complex ideas clearly, while continuing to practice coaching and inspiring the people I worked with. Inclusivity was a constant effort. Every cohort brought new people with different experiences and perspectives, so I wanted to keep getting better at understanding everyone I taught and making sure they got what they needed from the experience. I was excited to meet new people and facilitators and grow alongside them. One of the things I valued most in leadership was the relational side, the work of genuinely understanding one another. That's why I was looking forward to working with both new and returning faces this semester.
